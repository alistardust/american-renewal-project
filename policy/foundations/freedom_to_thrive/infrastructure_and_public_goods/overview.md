# Infrastructure and Public Goods — Overview

*This file is a stub. Narrative content is pending.*

The infrastructure of modern life — electrical grids, water systems, broadband internet, roads, bridges, and public transit — cannot be left to private extraction or allowed to decay through neglect. This pillar establishes public investment in shared infrastructure as a non-negotiable obligation of government, covering universal access standards, anti-monopoly rules for essential utilities, and the principle that essential systems must serve everyone.
