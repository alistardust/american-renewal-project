# Education — Policy Rules

## Scope Code: EDU

This document contains all Education policy rules organized by family code. These rules establish comprehensive standards for K-12 funding equity, higher education affordability, student debt relief, teacher workforce, civic and science education, secular public schooling, vocational pathways, anti-segregation measures, and governance accountability.
