# Labor & Workers' Rights — Policy Rules

## Scope Code: LAB

This document contains all Labor & Workers' Rights policy rules organized by family code. These rules establish comprehensive standards for wages, hours, leave, worker classification, collective bargaining, union rights, algorithmic management, workplace surveillance, safety, and enforcement.
