# Clean Democracy — Policy Pillars

The following policy domains are built on this foundation:

## Elections and Democratic Representation
The architecture of democratic legitimacy. Covers voting rights, redistricting reform, campaign finance limits, ranked-choice voting, equal access to the ballot, and protections against administrative or partisan sabotage of elections. Every eligible person must have an equal vote that carries equal weight.
→ [overview.md](./elections_and_representation/overview.md) | [policy rules](./elections_and_representation/policy.md)

## Anti-Corruption
Government must answer to the public, not to donors, dark-money networks, foreign influence operations, or shadow lobbying systems. Covers disclosure requirements, lobbying regulations, the revolving door, emoluments enforcement, conflicts of interest, and independent anti-corruption infrastructure with real enforcement power.
→ [overview.md](./anti_corruption/overview.md) | [policy rules](./anti_corruption/policy.md)

## Antitrust and Corporate Power
Prevents monopolistic control from undermining both economic fairness and democratic self-governance. Covers market concentration reform, platform regulation, corporate governance accountability, supply chain monopoly, and the relationship between economic power and political influence. When a handful of corporations control information, food, healthcare, or finance, they gain the power to set terms for everyone else — including government.
→ [overview.md](./antitrust_and_corporate_power/overview.md) | [policy rules](./antitrust_and_corporate_power/policy.md)

## Information and Media
Protects the flow of truthful information from power to the public while preventing coordinated manipulation, foreign interference, and the weaponization of information systems. Covers press freedom, public broadcasting infrastructure, broadband access, platform accountability, AI-enabled disinformation, and media ownership transparency. Open, honest information flow is a democratic prerequisite — not an optional amenity.
→ [overview.md](./information_and_media/overview.md) | [policy rules](./information_and_media/policy.md)
