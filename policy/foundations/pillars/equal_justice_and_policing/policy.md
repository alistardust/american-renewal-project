# Equal Justice and Policing — Policy Rules

## Scope Code: JUS

---

## AI — AI in Justice Systems

### JUS-AI-001 — Ban AI sentencing determinations
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI systems may not be used to determine or recommend criminal sentencing outcomes including incarceration length or punitive measures

### JUS-AI-002 — Ban AI recidivism predictions in sentencing
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI risk assessment tools predicting recidivism dangerousness or likelihood of reoffense may not be used in sentencing decisions

### JUS-AI-003 — AI for wrongful conviction identification
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI may be used to assist in identifying wrongful convictions procedural errors and case review opportunities to accelerate appeals and justice processes

### JUS-AI-004 — AI for bias identification under oversight
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI systems should be used to identify and mitigate bias in charging sentencing and judicial processes under strict oversight and transparency

### JUS-AI-005 — AI evidence transparency requirements
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI-generated evidence or analysis may not be used in court unless it is transparent auditable and subject to challenge by all parties

### JUS-AI-006 — Right to examine AI systems
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** Defendants must have the right to examine challenge and cross-examine any AI system or output used in legal proceedings

### JUS-AI-007 — Ban AI juror profiling
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI systems may not be used to profile rank or exclude jurors based on inferred characteristics behavioral patterns or demographic proxies

### JUS-AI-008 — Ban AI prosecutorial automation
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI systems may not be used to recommend or automate prosecutorial decisions such as charging strategies plea deals or case prioritization without human accountability and oversight

### JUS-AI-009 — Mandatory AI disclosure
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** All use of AI in legal proceedings must be disclosed to all parties including purpose limitations and methodology

### JUS-AI-010 — AI evidence validity standards
**Status:** MISSING
**Statement:** AI evidence must meet strict reliability and scientific validity standards equivalent to or exceeding existing evidentiary standards
