# Anti-Corruption, Political Influence, and Concentrated Power — Policy Rules

## Scope Code: COR

---

## ETH — Ethics and Lobbying

### COR-ETH-001 — Ban lobbyists from governing industries
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Ban lobbyists from governing industries  
**Notes:** Prevents regulatory capture where individuals who lobbied for an industry then move into positions regulating that same industry, creating direct conflicts of interest and undermining public trust in neutral governance.

### COR-ETH-002 — Ban stock trading by officials
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Ban stock trading by officials  
**Notes:** Prevents conflicts of interest where officials can use insider knowledge from their government roles to trade stocks, or where financial holdings create incentives to favor certain industries or companies in policy decisions.

### COR-ETH-003 — Applies to Congress
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Applies to Congress  
**Notes:** Stock trading and lobbying bans apply to all members of Congress, preventing legislators from profiting from their policy knowledge or industry relationships.

### COR-ETH-004 — Applies to judges
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Applies to judges  
**Notes:** Stock trading and lobbying bans apply to federal judges, ensuring judicial independence and preventing financial conflicts from influencing legal decisions.

### COR-ETH-005 — Applies to executive branch
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Applies to executive branch  
**Notes:** Stock trading and lobbying bans apply to executive branch officials, preventing conflicts of interest in regulatory and enforcement decisions.

### COR-ETH-006 — Applies to president and VP
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Applies to president and VP  
**Notes:** Stock trading and lobbying bans apply to the president and vice president, ensuring the highest offices are free from financial conflicts and cannot use their positions for personal enrichment.

### COR-ETH-007 — Applies to immediate family
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Applies to immediate family  
**Notes:** Stock trading and conflict-of-interest rules extend to immediate family members of covered officials to prevent circumvention through family proxies.

### COR-ETH-008 — Applies to spouses and partners
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Applies to spouses and partners  
**Notes:** Specifically includes spouses and domestic partners in conflict-of-interest protections to close common loopholes where officials claim no control over spouse's financial activities.

### COR-ETH-009 — Applies to extended relationships
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Applies to extended relationships  
**Notes:** Extends scrutiny to broader family and financial relationships where there is evidence of coordination or benefit-sharing, preventing evasion through extended networks.

### COR-ETH-010 — Allow only blind collective investment
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Allow only blind collective investment  
**Notes:** Permits covered officials to hold investments only in blind trusts or broad-based collective investment vehicles (like index funds) where they cannot influence or know specific holdings, ensuring complete separation from potential conflicts.

## FIN — Financial Conflicts and Campaign Finance

### COR-FIN-001 — Ban corporate political donations
**Status:** INCLUDED  
**Statement:** Ban corporate political donations  
**Notes:** Prohibits corporations from making political donations to candidates, parties, or political committees, addressing the core Citizens United problem where corporate money can dominate political processes and drown out individual voices.

### COR-FIN-002 — Ban Super PACs
**Status:** PARTIAL  
**Statement:** Ban Super PACs  
**Notes:** Eliminates Super PACs that allow unlimited political spending by individuals, unions, and corporations under the guise of "independence," closing a major channel for concentrated wealth to influence elections.

### COR-FIN-003 — Limit individual donations
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Limit individual donations  
**Notes:** Establishes strict caps on individual political donations to prevent wealthy individuals from exercising outsized influence through large contributions, ensuring more equal political voice.

### COR-FIN-004 — Limit political ad spending
**Status:** MISSING  
**Statement:** Limit political ad spending  
**Notes:** Considers limits on advertisement spending and broadcast/streaming political ad saturation to prevent elections from becoming pure spending contests and reduce the advantage wealth provides in shaping public discourse.

## WHB — White House and Executive Branch Ethics

### COR-WHB-001 — Whistleblower protections
**Status:** INCLUDED  
**Statement:** Whistleblower protections  
**Notes:** Provides strong legal protections for whistleblowers who expose corruption, fraud, abuse of power, or illegal conduct in government, ensuring they cannot be fired, demoted, harassed, or prosecuted in retaliation for reporting wrongdoing. Essential for enforcement because insiders are often the only ones with knowledge of hidden corruption.
